What To Expect When We Sand Your Floors

NOISE - DUST- VAPOR

Noise from the Machinery and Sanding Process

Dust from the Sanding

Vapors from the Sanding

Depending on the coatings used, there will be residual smell

Coating can take unto 7-10 days Full Cure.

You may need to touch up Paint after floors are finished and dust work areas.
WHAT WE REQUIRE

Good Clear Access.

Power Supply

Restricted access to sanding area form.

No access when floor is being coated

An area for bagging dust

No pilot lights or open flames when coating.

TYPES OR FINISHES
SOLVENT BASED URETHANE

A durable and tough finish available in Gloss, Satin or Matt. There is a strong vapor during application. the floor will darken with time, edgebonding may occur.

WATER BASED URETHANE

Durable - Low odour - Non toxic, available in Gloss, Satin or Matt.Maintains a more natural timber colour, reduces edgebonding.

OIL MODIFIED URETHANE

A combination of oils & urethane, better durability than oil based finishes, available in Gloss or Satin. Darkens with time, reduces edgebonding, slow to cure in cold weather.

OIL BASED FINISHES

Traditional types of finish, available in Gloss or Satin. Requires greater regular maintenance, darkens with time, reduces edgebonding, slow to cure in cold weather.

MAINTENANCE

Matting at external doors, regular sweeping or vacuuming, damp mopping with recommended cleaner, prompt removal of spillages, protective felt pads on furniture, recoating as required
